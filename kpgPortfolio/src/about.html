About Me

My name is Kyle Griffith, I'm 19, and sometimes I go by KP. I am a Front End Web Developer currently located in Birmingham, AL. What keeps me interested in learning and furthering my understanding of current Web Technologies is the satisifcation that comes from learning not so much the A B C's of coding, but the technique and technical approach required to yield not only the best results, but the most efficent results.

Originally, I wanted to pursue music, however COVID threw me a curveball and took me into a more long term and secure direction. When I took my first steps into Web Development, I took the self taught route, developed some bad habits, and made some cool websites --relative to the standards from the Dot-com bubble from the 90's/2000's. I decided if I wanted to pursue a career in this field I should lay down a solid foundation and have sound fundementals. By doing so, I chose to enroll in the Full Stack Web Development with PHP course through Jefferson State Community College here in Birmingham. From November, 2020, up to May of 2021, I received the opprotunity to see first hand the proper implementation of semantics as well as an experienced approach towards writing clean and reusable code.

Having two parents in the Air Force, I consider myself a Tennesseean after living in Nashville the longest out of anywhere I had lived previously. When I am not learning about Web Technologies, you can find me playing/tracking guitar, kayaking or waiting for it to be football season.
